Projects

Tic Tac Toe
A classic grade school passtime for those who want to prove to their friends that they can make 3 in a row first. However, sometimes finding a good sheet of paper, a freshly sharpened pencil, and a friend can be tough. To solve this, I wrote an AI so you don't have to worry about the aforementioned any longer! I've even given the AI an artificial delay to make it feel like there is actually another person playing!

For people without friends!
Checkers
A more competative and strategic game than Tic Tac Toe, Checkers is another great game that is hard to play without a friend. I decided to push the computing power of a single-threaded HTML page to its limits by porting my minimax algorithm from Tic Tac Toe to Checkers! With the optimization of Alpha-Beta pruning, the AI can look 11 moves ahead in order to pick the best next move. I think it works, but if someone a lot better at Checkers than me could find out, that would be great!

For people without friends!
Chess
